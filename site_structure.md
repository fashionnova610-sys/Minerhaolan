## Design System

### Color Palette
- **Primary Navy:** #1a2844 (header, hero backgrounds)
- **Secondary Blue:** #3b5998 (accents, buttons)
- **Light Gray:** #f5f5f5 (backgrounds)
- **White:** #ffffff (cards, text on dark)
- **Text Dark:** #333333
- **Text Light:** #666666
- **Accent Gold:** #ffa500 (badges, highlights)
- **Success Green:** #4caf50
- **Sale Red:** #ff4444

### Typography
- **Headings:** Sans-serif, bold, large sizes
- **Body Text:** Sans-serif, regular weight
- **Product Specs:** Monospace or sans-serif, smaller size

### Component Styles

**Product Cards:**
- White background
- Subtle shadow on hover
- Rounded corners (8-12px)
- Discount badge in top-right corner
- Specifications in table format
- Clear pricing display

**Buttons:**
- Primary: Dark blue with white text
- Secondary: White with dark border
- Hover states with subtle transitions
- Rounded corners

**Navigation:**
- Sticky header on scroll
- Dropdown menus with smooth transitions
- Mobile: Hamburger menu

### Responsive Breakpoints
- Mobile: < 768px
- Tablet: 768px - 1024px
- Desktop: > 1024px
- Wide: > 1440px

1. **Branding:** Replace all "Miners.de" with "MinerHaolan"
2. **Location:** Hong Kong-based company serving global customers
3. **Language:** Primary English interface (not German)
4. **Currency:** Support multiple currencies (USD, EUR, HKD, CNY)
5. **Shipping:** Worldwide shipping information
6. **Product Images:** Use placeholder images initially
7. **Contact Info:** Hong Kong address and contact details
8. **Legal Pages:** Adapt for Hong Kong jurisdiction
